Q1: What is investing?

A: Investing is putting money into assets (stocks, bonds, mutual funds) to grow it over time.

Q2: Difference between stocks and bonds?

A: Stocks = ownership in a company (higher risk, higher reward), Bonds = lending money to a company/government (lower risk, fixed returns).

Q3: What is compound interest?

A: Interest earned on both your initial investment and the interest it has already earned.

Q4: Risk vs reward?

A: Higher potential returns usually come with higher risk. Always balance according to your goal and time horizon.

Q5: Tips for smart investing?

A: Start early, diversify your portfolio, and invest regularly.
