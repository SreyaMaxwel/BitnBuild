Q1: What is debt?

A: Debt is money you borrow from someone (bank, credit card) that you must pay back, usually with interest.

Q2: Good vs bad debt?

A: Good debt builds value (education, home), bad debt is for things that lose value (expensive gadgets, parties).

Q3: How does interest work?

A: Interest is extra money you pay on top of the borrowed amount. The higher the interest, the more expensive the debt.

Q4: How to manage debt?

A: Pay on time, avoid unnecessary loans, and prioritize high-interest debts first.

Q5: Tips to stay debt-free?

A: Spend within limits, save for emergencies, and only borrow when necessary.
